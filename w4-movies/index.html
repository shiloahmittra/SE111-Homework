Skyscraper

Skyscraper is about an FBI Hostage Rescue Team leader who works as a security consultant for the world's tallest and most advanced skyscarper, The Pearl. It is located in Hong Kong where a group of terrorists set the skyscraper on fire, attempting to frame Will Sawyer for the attack. Will fights for the life of himself and his family who lives in the tower.

Company Credits: Legendary Pictures, Flynn Picture Company, Seven Bucks Productions, & Universal Pictures.

Release Date: July 13, 2018

Genres: Action, Adventure, & Thriller

Rating: PG-13

Running Time: 102 minutes
