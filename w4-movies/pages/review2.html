Review

Shiloah Mittra

April 20, 2026

A Man Called Otto is a very emotional movie of a man who was once a kind-hearted gentleman. He was changed from the passing of his beloved wife, hurting from depression. He turned into a grumpy old man who was hated by the community. When he got new neighbors, his old self was slowly rescued. Though he remained grump initally, his inner self poured out kindness to his neighbors which was very touching.

From Otto's depression, he had three failed suicidal attempts. Though a sad situation, this was slightly humourous as no matter how hard he tried, he could never succeed in ending his life. His first attempt was by hanging himself, but the ceiling ripped right out. His second attempt was trying to shoot himself, but a very loud sound disrupted him last second, causing him to miss the shot. His final attempt, almost succeeding, was by gassing himself inside a car. He was found within minutes, and his life was saved yet again. Toward the end of the movie, his grumpy self had left and his kind-heart had returned in full capacity. The ending was very emotional as he later fell sick and passed away from a disease that made him have a very big heart. He was dearly missed by his new neighbors, but they were glad they had saved him no matter what.
