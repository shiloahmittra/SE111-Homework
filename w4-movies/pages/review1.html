Review

Shiloah Mittra

April 20, 2026

Skyscraper is a very fast paced movie that quickly escalates. The main charater played by Dwayne Johnson partakes in many stunts throughout the movie which add a high level of thrill for the audience. The main character, Will Sawyer, is a security expert, trapped on the outside of the world's tallest skyscraper. He does his absolute best to not only save himself, but also his family as the terrorist set the tower on fire.

The Skyscraper in the movie houses many guests, including the Sawyer family. The building is very technically advanced and has the abiliy to alert Will's family about the fire. My favorite scene in the movie was where Will's family had to use a disabled elevator as their only chances of escaping the burning building. This climb in, count a specifc time, then pull a level to activate a braking system. This scene was very intense as miscounting would cause them to fatally crash in the elevator.
